# A Perseverance pakolja majd egy rakétára marsi mintáit, hogy azok visszajuthassanak a Földre

# Az amerikai és az európai űrűgynökség tegnapi bejelentése leegyszerűsítette a marsi minta-visszahozatalt, aminek a célja kideríteni, hogy kialakult-e a valaha a bolygón az élet.

Egyszerűbben és olcsóbban juthatnak földi laboratóriumokba a Perseverance marsjáró által gyűjtött kőzetminták. A legújabb tervek szerint azokat nem egy újabb marsjáró, hanem a Perseverance vagy helikopterek szállítják majd egy rakétához, ami 2031-ben elindítja Föld felé vezető útjukra őket, 2033-as érkezésre - derül ki a NASA és az ESA [tegnapi](https://www.nasa.gov/press-release/nasa-esa-to-discuss-mars-sample-return-mission) sajtótájékoztatójából.

Az amerikai és európai űrűgynökségek több milliárd dolláros marsi minta-visszahozatali programjának célja kideríteni, hogy létezett-e a bolygón valaha élet.

- Thomas Zurbuchen (NASA, Science Mission Directorate associate admin)

Mission is happening as we speak. Perseverance collecting samples. MSRP nearing completion in concepctual design phase. During this phase number of configs were examined for fetching the samples to the ascent vehicle lander.

Second lander for sample fetch rover was added. We are learning about Perserverance as well. Revised and innovative architecture. New studies and recent achievements. We considered new results that werent available a year ago.

New archictecture relies on perseverance as primary system. esa sample arm will load samples to american mars ascent vehicle. two ingenuity class helicopters as backup to transport samples to lander.

- David Parker (ESA, human and robotic exploration director)

Very excinting program, very important partnerhship. Unique mission. Priceless scientific treasure from the red planet, will be studied for the next 50 years, fundemantal questions on Mars and history. Absolute age dating of rocks from known geological contects. Stepping stone to human mission to mars. multi element, complex mission. ESA's eaerth return orbiter being built now, multi stage spacecraft, chemical and solar propulsion, more powerful than ever used. radiation sensor HUNGARY total radiation dose measured on the way, at Mars and back home. assess risk for future human explorers.

- Jeff Gramling (MSRP director NASA)

Significant milestone in the campaign. Architecture begun with Perseverance. Already collected 9 scientifically selected samples, ready to be brought home. Perseverance relaibility and life expectancy (+ Curiosity 10th year) was assessed based on updated assessment, rover will be available in 2030 to deliver samples. 29 flights ingenuity - jpl study evaluted the concept of helicopters as sample delivery. confident on support role as backup to perseverance. one lander will carry 2 helicopters, mars ascent vehicle, esa sample transfer arm. sample arm will get the samples from perseverance. single sample retrieval lander. ascent vehichle. nasa provided capture and containment system, esa earth return orbiter. 

september next decision point, finalizing architecture.

- Francois Spoto (ESA Mars exploration group)

randevous with orbiting samples at Mars. return to Utah in 2033. esa sample arm will grab samples from perseverance - adapted from perseverance and helicopter.

[Meenakshi Wadhwa](https://news.asu.edu/20210415-asu-professor-named-nasa-mars-sample-return-program-scientist), sample return principle scientist. - incredible sample collected yd. fine grain sedimentary rocks - greatest chance for preserving biosignatures.

two helicopters like ingenuity in terms of size and mass. landing legs with small wheels - traverse across the surface across the ground. helicopters will have a little arm to grab tubes.

price saving, time saving: plan always has been to use perseverance. architecture revison based on reliabality assessment. simpler, less complex, we have a simpler architecture. money saved: less expensive.

date: planned arrival mid-2030, mav launch 2031, return to sample 2033.

SRL - sample retrieval lander, pinpoint landing capability, will get close

Basic plan for Prsv to contiunue Jezero delta campaign, samples put into first depo later this year, after drive up the rest of the delta, than go out of the crater into the midway site, very ancient terrain. Perseverance is carrying the PRIMARY samples. If its breaking down, second sample depo will be made. Depo in Jezero will remain on Mars if all goes to plan.

SRL could land where Perseverance ends up. First depo within Jezero. Perseverance collecting some duplicates, those will be carried within Perseverance to outside the crater.
